About Me

I am associate professor in computer science at Centrale MÃ©diterranÃ©e in Marseille (France). I'm specialized in the environmental footprint of modern AI with a particular interest for deep learning audio models.

I did my Ph.D. at IRCAM in Paris (STMS laboratory, ACIDS team) under the supervision of Philippe Esling and Jean-Pierre Briot, where I studied the energy consumption of neural networks applied to music generation.
