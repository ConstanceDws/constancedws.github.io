About Me

I am Associate Professor in Computer Science. My academic journey began with a Ph.D. at IRCAM (STMS laboratory, ACIDS team), where I studied the energy consumption of neural networks applied to audio generation.

After that, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at INRIA Nancy (Multispeech team), focusing on the environmental impacts of AI systems used for sound event detection.

I am now a member of the QARMA team at the LIS laboratory, continuing to explore the environmental footprint of AI systems.
